## neo4j scan timeout at 300k+ Vulnerability nodes

`MATCH ({stix_id: $x})` does a full graph scan. fine at 10k nodes, dies at
300k+. fast_attack_rels.py rewrites every MATCH to use a label:

  MATCH (v:Vulnerability {stix_id: $x})
  MATCH (t:AttackPattern {ext_id: $y})

with the label, neo4j hits the schema index instead of scanning. relationship
build went from "timeout after 600s" to ~4 minutes for the full corpus.

lesson: always label your MATCH at scale. don't trust the planner to figure it
out from constraints alone.
